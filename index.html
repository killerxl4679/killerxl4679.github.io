System Development Life Cycle(SDLC)

SDCL Phases And SDLC Roles

SDLC Phase Description SDCL Roles
Idea Idea is when you come up with something and think about what you want it to be. Project manager
Resources Resources can be money or the people in the group that will help you build the idea. Business Owner
Design its when you have Visual Design skills and have an envisioning to the project. UI Designer
Planning planning involves Strategy and having a thought process Technical Architect
Build/Test Coding expirience, analizing, patient, methodical Computer scientist
Publish When you release the project/product Testing Engineer
Bugs Future requests on how to improve the Idea and making it better. Devops Engineer


SDLC ROLES Description
Project manager the person in overall charge of the planning and execution of a particular projec
Business Owner A business owner is one person who is in control of the operational and monetary aspects of a business.
UI Designer someone who designs the graphical user interface of an app, website, or device that someone can interact with.
Technical Architect specialized, hands-on role that is concerned with the development
Computer Scientist Someone who specializes in computer science and work on the theoretical side of computation.
Testing Engineer Testing engineer is someone who creates a process that would best test a particular product in manufacturing and related disciplines, in order to assure that the product meets applicable specifications.
Devops Engineer A person who works with both development and operations teams to implement software releases and deployments.
